Spaghetti

A delicious and simple dish

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti pasta
  • Salt
  • Olive oil
  • Mushrooms
  • Turkey meat

Steps

  1. Boil water in a pot and add salt and olive oir
  2. Add the spaghetti pasta and wait for 10 minutes
  3. Heat olive oil in a "paella" and heat the mushrooms and dices of turkey meat
